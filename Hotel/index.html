About

Serena Hotel a (5.6K) • 5-star hotel

This high-end hotel is 3 km from the Uganda Museum, 4 km from Kabaka's Palace and 6 km from Kasubi Tombs.

  • Elegant rooms offer free Wi-Fi, satellite TV and minibars, plus safes, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add sitting areas. Room service is available.
  • Breakfast is complimentary. Dining options consist of 2 restaurants, a bakery and a bistro, plus 2 bars and a cafe. A tranquil spa offers plunge pools, saunas and hammams, plus a lounge. Other amenities include 2 outdoor pools (1 for kids), a gym, and meeting and event space.
