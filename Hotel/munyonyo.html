About

Munyonyo Commonwealth Resort a 4.5 • 5-star hotel

Set on 90 acres of lush gardens overlooking Lake Victoria, this serene, upmarket resort is a 15 minutes’ walk from Munyonyo Martyrs’ Shrine and 13 km from the Independence Monument. Entebbe International Airport is 44 km away.

  • Colourful rooms feature Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs and minifridges, as well as in-room safes, and balconies with garden or lake views. Suites add kitchenettes and separate living areas. A thatched bi-level cottage sleeps up to 4 guests. In-room dining is available 24/7.
